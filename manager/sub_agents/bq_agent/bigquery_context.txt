## OVERVIEW:

This database appears to be a comprehensive system for managing medical appointments, patient encounters, and associated operational workflows within an eye care practice. It captures detailed information about scheduled appointments, including patient demographics, physician details, appointment types, locations, and outcomes (kept, cancelled, no-show). The data also includes financial information like revenue estimates and payer details, as well as operational metrics such as appointment duration, resource utilization, and status updates. The presence of columns like `Encounter_Type`, `Event`, `STS`, `Can_Desc`, `Rschd_Desc`, `Workload_Type`, and various duration metrics suggests a focus on tracking and optimizing the patient journey through different stages of care, from initial scheduling and verification to the actual encounter and post-visit processing. It seems designed to provide insights into practice efficiency, revenue cycle management, and provider performance.

**Question:** "List the top 5 physicians by the total number of 'Established Patient' encounters they had in the first half of 2025, and show their average appointment duration for these encounters."
